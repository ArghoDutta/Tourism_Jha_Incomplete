    About Us

    Jharkhand, the eastern heartland of India, is a land of breathtaking natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and spiritual significance. With lush forests, cascading waterfalls, sacred peaks, wildlife reserves, ancient temples, and vibrant tribal traditions, the state offers an unmatched palette for every kind of traveller. Recognised for its biodiversity, historical monuments, and indigenous cultures, Jharkhand has made tourism a priority industry, developing circuits and facilities to welcome visitors with warmth, safety, and infrastructure. Whether you seek adventure in its hills, peace in its pilgrim sites, or immersion in its folk arts, Jharkhand promises an experience both deeply authentic and refreshingly off the beaten track.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to position Jharkhand as a leading and sustainable tourism destination by showcasing its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant traditions. We are committed to developing world-class infrastructure, promoting eco-friendly practices, and creating opportunities for local communities through responsible tourism. By preserving our forests, waterfalls, wildlife, and sacred sites while offering safe and memorable experiences to visitors, we aim to make Jharkhand a preferred choice for domestic and international travellers alike.

    Who We Are

    The Tourism Department of Jharkhand Government is dedicated to developing and promoting tourism in the state. We work tirelessly to highlight Jharkhand's unique attractions including its majestic waterfalls, ancient temples, tribal culture, and abundant wildlife.

    Our Vision

    To establish Jharkhand as a world-class eco-tourism destination that preserves its natural heritage while providing memorable experiences to visitors from around the globe.
