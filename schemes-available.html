    Government Tourism Schemes

    Discover exclusive benefits and offers designed to make your Jharkhand journey more affordable and memorable. The Government of Jharkhand is committed to promoting tourism through various welfare schemes.

    Women Safety Accommodation

    Free Stay

    Special accommodation facility for women travelers to ensure safe and comfortable stay in Jharkhand.

    Benefits Available:

    • Free accommodation for up to 2 women in a single family
    • Safe and secure government-approved lodges
    • 24/7 security and assistance
    • Clean and hygienic facilities

    Eligibility:

    Women travelers (any age) visiting Jharkhand for tourism purposes

    Senior Citizen Tourism Scheme

    40% Off

    Special discounts for senior citizens to encourage elderly tourism and make travel more affordable.

    Benefits Available:

    • 40% discount on accommodation costs
    • Reduced entry fees at tourist attractions
    • Priority booking and assistance
    • Medical support availability

    Eligibility:

    Tourists aged 60 years and above with valid age proof

    Extended Stay GST Relief Scheme

    5% GST Cut

    Encouraging longer stays in Jharkhand by providing GST relief for extended tourism visits.

    Benefits Available:

    • 5% GST reduction on total tourism expenses
    • Applicable on accommodation, food, and activities
    • Easy claim process through registered vendors
    • Digital receipt and refund system

    Eligibility:

    Tourists planning to stay for more than 10 days in Jharkhand

    Emergency Medical Support

    15% Coverage

    Comprehensive medical support for tourists facing health emergencies during their visit to Jharkhand.

    Benefits Available:

    • Government covers up to 15% of medical costs
    • 24/7 emergency medical helpline
    • Network of approved hospitals and clinics
    • Ambulance services in remote areas

    Eligibility:

    All registered tourists with valid ID and tourism registration

    Student Group Tourism Scheme

    30% Off

    Educational tourism promotion scheme for student groups to explore Jharkhand's heritage and nature.

    Benefits Available:

    • 30% discount on group bookings (minimum 15 students)
    • Free guided tours at historical sites
    • Educational material and souvenirs
    • Special transport arrangements

    Eligibility:

    Student groups from recognized educational institutions with teacher supervision

    Tribal Culture Experience

    Cultural Package

    Authentic tribal culture experience with local communities while supporting indigenous livelihoods.

    Benefits Available:

    • Homestay with tribal families
    • Traditional food and cultural programs
    • Handicraft workshops and shopping
    • 20% discount on cultural activities

    Eligibility:

    Tourists interested in cultural immersion with advance booking

    Eco-Tourism Incentive Scheme

    Green Bonus

    Promoting sustainable tourism practices with incentives for eco-friendly travel choices.

    Benefits Available:

    • 25% discount on eco-lodge accommodations
    • Free nature guide services
    • Carbon offset certificates
    • Complimentary organic meals

    Eligibility:

    Tourists choosing eco-friendly accommodation and transport options

    Festival Season Special Scheme

    Festival Offer

    Special packages during major festivals to experience Jharkhand's vibrant cultural celebrations.

    Benefits Available:

    • Festival package deals with 35% savings
    • VIP access to cultural events
    • Traditional costume rental included
    • Festival food and souvenir packages

    Eligibility:

    Tourists visiting during Sohrai, Chhath, Christmas, and other major festivals
